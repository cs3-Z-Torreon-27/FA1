Basketball

  • Basketball is a game played with two teams
  • Each team has 5 players
  • A team can score two points if scored in the three point line, and a team can score three points if they can shoot a ball outside the three point line
  • The sport consists of four quarters, each containing 12 minutes.
  • The team who has the most points by the end of the fourth quarter wins.
  • In case of a tie, the game will go into overtime that lasts for five minutes.
