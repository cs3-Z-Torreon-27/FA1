Volleyball

  • Volleyball is a game played with two teams
  • Each team has 6 players
  • A team can score a point if the ball touches the ground of the other team's side of the court
  • The team who reaches 25 points wins a set
  • The team who wins 3 sets first wins
  • If both teams have a score of 24, the first team to get a two point advantage wins
